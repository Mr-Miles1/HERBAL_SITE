Our Story

The SARFO YEBOAH GROUP is a family business that produces quality edible foods, herbal, alcoholic, and non-alcoholic beverages, personal care products, and also deals in the sale of both residential and farmlands across the country.

The company is situated in Fise-Amasaman in the Greater Accra Region of Ghana. It became operational in 2013 with the sale of office supplies until 2017, when the mobile money agency was added to our line of business. In 2018, farming was added to our line of business. We invested in both crop and animal farming in the then Brong-Ahafo Region.

The Sarfo Yeboah group has gradually etched itself into the Ghanaian herbal and beverage market by manufacturing and marketing high-quality products to meet our customers' needs.

Sarfo Yeboah Herbal Research and Clinic was established in 2023 by T/Dr. SARFO YEBOAH to realize the passion and vision of the talented entrepreneurs.

Though T/Dr. Sarfo Yeboah believed in Traditional and Alternative medicine; he did not pay much attention to it until the death of her sister in 2022. Since then, he vowed to promote traditional medicine after the orthodox treatment failed to treat his beloved sister.

Sarfo Yeboah Herbal Research and Clinic is primarily into the manufacturing of herbal products, diagnosing, and treating various ailments with herbs. We also specialize in disease prevention through health education and screenings. We focus on the provision of safe, effective, quality, and well-packaged herbal medicines that are sustainable.
